About The Drafting Company

 

Since opening our doors in 1993, The Drafting Company has focused its attention on providing CAD drafting and 3D modeling services to building construction and manufacturing related clients and their projects throughout the US and worldwide. Diversely supporting AEC building construction in Architectural, Structural, Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, Fire Protection trade services and manufacturing industries. Serving the continuously growing needs of AutoCAD, Revit, MicroStation, Tekla, SolidWorks, Inventor, Creo…etc. based markets from Hawaii to New York, and everywhere in-between, we typically offer our services virtually from hundreds to thousands of miles away. We offer local support in about a dozen major metropolitan areas.

 

Our teams possess a mastery of leading-edge technologies that allows us to offer basic to advanced levels of service. We understand that every client has unique requirements and projects. To best match our teams to these we have instilled a culture that is flexible and strives to adhere to quality demands, project schedules and budgetary constraints. We are dedicated to meeting project goals, committed to developing long-lasting business relationships by providing excellent customer service.

A Brief History

1993

The Drafting Company, TDC opens, focusing on providing a variety of drafting services and support to manufacturers and building construction trades.

2002

TDC adds Inventor and SolidWorks 3D modeling services to its growing list of offerings.

2007

We expanded our sales coverage areas to include Houston, Los Angeles, New York, and St. Louis.

2010

Added Revit Building Information Modeling (BIM) service capabilities within Autodesk’s AEC software package suite of products, to further assist and support in the growing needs of architectural, structural, mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and fire protection construction disciplines.

2017

TDC more than doubled its nationwide sales coverage areas to include Colorado, Dallas, Fort Worth, Honolulu, Las Vegas, Miami, Tampa Bay, St. Petersburg, Phoenix and Washington DC.
