A Brief History

1993

The Drafting Company, TDC opens, focusing on providing a variety of drafting services and support to manufacturers and building construction trades.

2002

TDC adds Inventor and SolidWorks 3D modeling services to its growing list of offerings.

2007

We expanded our sales coverage areas to include Houston, Los Angeles, New York, and St. Louis.

2010

Added Revit Building Information Modeling (BIM) service capabilities within Autodesk’s AEC software package suite of products, to further assist and support in the growing needs of architectural, structural, mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and fire protection construction disciplines.

2017

TDC more than doubled its nationwide sales coverage areas to include Colorado, Dallas, Fort Worth, Honolulu, Las Vegas, Miami, Tampa Bay, St. Petersburg, Phoenix and Washington DC.
